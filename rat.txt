Common Rat (Rattus)

Characteristics:
- Highly intelligent and adaptable rodents
- Strong swimmers and climbers
- Excellent sense of smell and hearing
- Size ranges from 15-40 cm in length (excluding tail)
- Capable of squeezing through very small openings

Habitat:
Rats are found worldwide in various environments, including:
- Urban areas and cities
- Agricultural regions
- Forests and woodlands
- Human settlements
- Underground burrows and sewers

Behavior:
- Nocturnal animals, most active at night
- Highly social, living in colonies
- Quick learners and problem solvers
- Excellent memory for navigation
- Communicate through various vocalizations and pheromones
- Known for their grooming habits and cleanliness
